American Hertiage Girls

American Heritage Girls is a scouting organization for ages 5 to 18. It teaches them leadership and job skills.

I was in AHG from ages 13 to 16 or grades 7 to 10. During my time in the program I recived many badges that trained me in many diffrent skills and abilites from bike saftey to cake decorating. I learned many things at many diffrent events, I learned how to preform first aid and CPR. I took a first responders course, a lifeguard course and test, and self defense and so much more. I had the chance to not just perticipate in service projects I have sponsered a blood drive for the past 4 years and will continue to do so.
